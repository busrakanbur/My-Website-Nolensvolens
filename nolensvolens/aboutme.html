About Me

I love art, aesthetics, trees and cats. I love capturing all of this while living.

4th year computer engineering student... part time artist... photographer...
Someone who has a lot of fun trying to be good in many areas.
I have been involved with photography for a long time. But my only memory that can be considered professional is the work I did in TedxAnkara. Being behind the camera, capturing the best in front of the eye; it's a lot like drawing a picture. I feel more free while drawing. It's like photographing your own dream scene. with your hands. I decided to sell the drawings I made first, on the products. I designed bags, t-shirts, mugs for a while. Then I decided to combine my designs with the software field. I started this job by making a web application. I also took a lesson about it. Then I started designing this site. There are still many missing parts, but I will improve.
